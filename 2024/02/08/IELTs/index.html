IELTS Preparation

Here is something I prepared for my IELTS test.

Words

  • due

    He got his due reward. Adj. 应得的报酬。

    Your English paper is due. Adj. 到期了。

  • lay

  • hurry - haste n. - hasty

    Hasty adj. 草率的 急躁的

    Hurry is a more commendatory word than hasty. 褒义的

  • urge: n. I have the urge to pursue a Ph.D. / v. They urge me to pass my IELTS test. n. 强烈欲望 迫切需求 v.催促 力劝

  • prompt v. 促使/ 提示 （给演员提示词）adj. 迅速的

    To prompt someone to do something means to make them decide to do it. 促使

    “You wouldn’t have wanted to bring those people to justice anyway, would you?” Brand prompted him.

    提示

    If you are prompt to do something, you do it without delay or you are not late. (做某事) 迅速的

  • promote v. 促进 / 促销 / 晋升

    You don’t have to sacrifice environmental protection to promote economic growth.
    你们没有必要牺牲环保来促进经济增长。

    Is it ethical to promote cigarettes through advertising?
    通过广告推销香烟合乎道德吗？

    I was promoted to editor and then editorial director.
    我晋升为编辑，之后又晋升为编辑部主任。

  • organize v.组织 安排 - arrange - assign v. 给..安排工作 / 布置任务 给..赋予能力

If you assign a piece of work to someone, you give them the work. 布置 (任务);

When I taught, I would assign a topic to children that they would write about.
教书时，我会给孩子们布置个写作题。

If you assign something to someone, you say that it is for their use. 分配 (某物)

If you assign a particular function or value to someone or something, you say they have it. 赋予 (某功能或价值)

Under the system, each business must assign a value to each job.
在这种制度下，每家企业必须赋予每份工作某种价值。

  • Resign v.辞退

  • credit

  • credentials n. 资格证书 资历

    I can testify to his credentials. 我可以证明他的资历。

  • critical adj. 至关重要的 危急的 批判性的

  • criterion - criteria(复数) n. 判断的指标

    The most important criterion for entry is that applicants(申请人) must design and make their own work.

  • Capitalism n. 资本主义 Socialism n.社会主义 Communism n. 共产主义 anarchism n. 无政府主义

    Anarchist n. 无政府主义者

  • Mushroom n. 蘑菇 v. 快速增长

    The population mushroomed in the postwar decades.

  • displace v. 取代 代替

  • replace

  • sew - stitch - weave - knit
