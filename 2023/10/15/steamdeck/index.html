SteamDeck

SteamDeck is my favorite handheld, bigger, heavier, and much more powerful. I spend a lot of time on Stardrew Valley with my NS, but the game’s life is always too short. After finishing all missions, there is nothing to do except make your farm look better. Mods can help with game content and SteamDeck is the only one satisfied.

Here is something I have done to my deck, which I am noting down to prevent forgetting.

CLI Configuration

The first thing you need to know is SteamOs is a publish version of Arch Linux, So if you want to install some software, just search in Google arch Linux.

Pacman
